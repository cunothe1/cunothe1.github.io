Welcome

Hello there! Welcome to my site. Here you can find out a bit more about me and what I do. This site will be the centre of my passion project. Here I’ll post blender renders, programs and other things that I enjoy! My goal is to share my creations and to keep busy with doing that what I love, coding, 3d-modelling and a bit of traveling. Not everything will be great here. The site could use some upgrades, but there is a reason. I want this site to be mine, and not some creation by wix, squarespace or any other website builders. I’m the builder here and I welcome you to my workplace!

Guitar

Music is one of my favourite ways to express myself. However, guitar is so beautiful that it would be a waste to only play originals. So I'm learning scores that I like from the youtube channel "Sky guitar". They post really good tutorials with tablature that is really detailed and totally not like the basic tablature you often find on guitar tabs. They do a real good job at keeping the soul of the pieces without throwing new players against a difficulty wall.
